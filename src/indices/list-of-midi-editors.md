# List of MIDI Editors

Listed here are MIDI editors in no particular order with several information. Some of which are used by blackers for making black MIDIs. The programs listed here are not necessarily optimized for black MIDIs.

<!-- HTML annotated tables are more readable in my IMO opinion desu --->

## Types
- Dedicated: editors that were developed specifically for editing MIDIs 
- Integrated: programs that coincidentally have MIDI editing capabilities
